

### **3. Pattern Overlay:**
```tsx
<div className="absolute inset-0 bg-[url('data:image/svg+xml...')] opacity-30"></div>
```

## 💡 **DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR FUTURE FEATURES**

When adding new features, follow these principles:

### **1. Use Gradients Generously**
Every important element should have a gradient:
- Headers: `bg-gradient-to-br from-[color] to-[color]`
- Buttons: `bg-gradient-to-r from-[color] to-[color]`
- Icons: Gradient backgrounds with white icons
- Borders: Gradient borders with white inner card

### **2. Add Depth with Shadows**
- Normal state: `shadow-lg`
- Hover state: `shadow-xl`
- Active elements: `shadow-2xl`
- Combine with borders for extra depth

### **3. Round Everything**
- Cards: `rounded-3xl` (24px)
- Buttons: `rounded-2xl` (16px)
- Icons: `rounded-xl` (12px)
- Badges: `rounded-full`

### **4. Animate Interactions**
- Hover: `hover:scale-110 transition-transform`
- Active: `group-hover:translate-x-1`
- Loading: `animate-pulse`
- Success: Fade in with `transition-opacity`

### **5. Use White Space**
- Section gaps: `space-y-6` minimum
- Card padding: `p-6` minimum
- Grid gaps: `gap-4` minimum
- Don't cram content
