## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### **Getting Started**

#### **What is goPay?**
goPay is Tanzania's all-in-one super app for payments, travel, shopping, government services, and more. Send money, pay bills, book flights, earn rewards - all in one app!

#### **How do I create an account?**
1. Download goPay from App Store or Google Play
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter your phone number
4. Verify with OTP code
5. Complete your profile (name, email, NIDA)
6. Set your 4-digit PIN
7. Start using goPay!

#### **Is goPay safe?**
Yes! We use:
- Bank-grade encryption
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Biometric login (fingerprint/Face ID)
- Fraud detection systems
- Bank of Tanzania compliance
- 24/7 security monitoring

#### **What do I need to sign up?**
- Valid Tanzania phone number
- Email address
- NIDA (National ID) number
- Age 18 or older
- Proof of address (for higher limits)

### **Wallet & Money**

#### **How do I add money to my wallet?**
1. Open goPay app
2. Tap "Wallet" > "Add Money"
3. Enter amount
4. Choose source (M-Pesa, bank, card, etc.)
5. Enter your PIN
6. Confirm transaction
7. Money added instantly!

**Supported sources:**
- M-Pesa
- Airtel Money
- Tigo Pesa
- Halo Pesa
- Bank accounts
- Visa/Mastercard

#### **How do I send money?**
1. Tap "Send Money"
2. Enter recipient's phone number
3. Enter amount
4. Add message (optional)
5. Enter PIN
6. Confirm
7. Money sent!

**Fees:**
- goPay to goPay: FREE
- To mobile money: TZS 500 + 1%
- To bank: TZS 1,000 + 0.5%

#### **What are the transaction limits?**
**Daily Limits:**
- Send: TZS 5,000,000
- Add: TZS 10,000,000
- Bills: TZS 5,000,000

**Single Transaction:**
- Max: TZS 5,000,000
- Min: TZS 100

*Limits increase with verification level*

#### **How long do transactions take?**
- goPay to goPay: Instant
- To mobile money: 1-5 minutes
- To bank: 10 minutes - 24 hours
- International: 1-3 business days

#### **Can I cancel a transaction?**
Once sent, most transactions cannot be canceled. Contact support immediately if you sent to wrong number.

**Refunds possible if:**
- Recipient hasn't claimed funds
- Within 7 days
- Valid reason provided

### **Bill Payments**

#### **What bills can I pay?**
- **Utilities:** TANESCO (electricity), DAWASCO (water), LUKU (prepaid meters)
- **Internet:** Vodacom, Airtel, Tigo, Halotel, Smile
- **TV:** DStv, Azam TV, StarTimes, Zuku
- **Government:** School fees, traffic fines, tax payments, municipal fees
- **Insurance:** Jubilee, Britam, AAR, Resolution, etc.
- **Loans:** Bank loans, SACCOS, microfinance

#### **How do I pay bills?**
1. Tap "Pay Bills"
2. Select category (utility, internet, etc.)
3. Choose provider
4. Enter account/reference number
5. Enter amount (or auto-filled)
6. Review details
7. Enter PIN
8. Payment complete!
9. Receipt sent via SMS & email

#### **Are there fees for bill payments?**
- Standard: TZS 300 per payment
- Silver members: FREE
- Gold/Platinum: FREE

### **Security & Privacy**

#### **Is my money safe?**
Yes! Your funds are protected by:
- Bank of Tanzania regulations
- Segregated accounts (your money is separate)
- Insurance coverage
- Fraud monitoring
- Encryption

#### **What if someone steals my phone?**
**Immediately:**
1. Call 24/7 hotline: +255 XX XXX XXXX
2. We'll freeze your account instantly
3. File police report
4. Get new phone & SIM
5. We'll help you recover your account

**Prevention:**
- Enable biometric login
- Use strong PIN
- Don't share PIN with anyone
- Enable 2FA
- Register device

#### **How do I enable 2FA?**
1. Settings > Security
2. Tap "Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Choose method (SMS or app)
4. Follow setup instructions
5. Verify with test code
6. 2FA enabled!

#### **What is SIM swap protection?**
We detect if your SIM card is changed and:
- Freeze your account temporarily
- Send alert to email
- Require additional verification
- Prevent unauthorized access

#### **How do I report fraud?**
**Immediately contact:**
- Email: fraud@gopay.tz
- Phone: +255 XX XXX XXXX (24/7)
- In-app: Settings > Security > Report Fraud

**We'll investigate and:**
- Freeze suspicious transactions
- Refund if fraud confirmed
- Report to authorities
- Update security measures

### **Technical Issues**

#### **App won't open / crashes**
Try:
1. Restart your phone
2. Update goPay to latest version
3. Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > goPay > Clear Cache)
4. Reinstall app
5. Still not working? Contact support

#### **Transaction failed**
Check:
- Internet connection
- Sufficient balance
- Correct recipient details
- Transaction limits not exceeded
- Service provider not down

**Still failed?** Transaction history shows error details. Contact support with transaction ID.

#### **Can't log in**
**Forgot PIN:**
1. Tap "Forgot PIN"
2. Verify with OTP
3. Set new PIN

**Forgot password:**
1. Tap "Forgot Password"
2. Enter email/phone
3. Receive reset link
4. Create new password

**Account locked:**
- Too many failed attempts
- Contact support to unlock

#### **Push notifications not working**
1. Settings > Notifications
2. Enable "Allow Notifications"
3. iOS: Settings > goPay > Notifications > Allow
4. Android: Settings > Apps > goPay > Notifications > Enable
